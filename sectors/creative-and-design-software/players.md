# Creative & design software — players

May 2026 market caps approximate.

## Tier 1 — must understand

| Ticker | Company | Market cap | Positioning | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| ADBE | Adobe | ~$97B | Category incumbent across Creative Cloud, Document Cloud, Experience Cloud | Largest, most diversified workflow owner. The bellwether. Mid-de-rate on AI-disruption narrative — single most asymmetric setup in mega-cap software. |
| ADSK | Autodesk | ~$45B | CAD/BIM incumbent (AutoCAD, Revit, Maya, Fusion) | The other mature workflow incumbent. Slower AI disruption clock (engineering files are harder to AI-generate than visuals). |
| FIG | Figma | ~$30B (post-IPO, evolving) | Web-native multiplayer product design | The AI-era-native challenger that's both public and material. Killed Adobe XD. Test case for whether browser-multiplayer architecture beats desktop incumbent. |

## Tier 3 — only if relevant

- **Corel / Affinity (private/PE-owned).** Perennial budget alternative to Adobe. Affinity (now part of Canva via 2024 acquisition) is the most credible challenger at the pro/prosumer tier on a one-time-purchase model.
- **Procreate (private, Australia).** Tablet-native paint/illustration; cultural niche.
- **Sketch (private, Netherlands).** Pre-Figma incumbent in product design; mostly displaced.
- **OpenAI (private).** Not a creative software company per se but the most important infrastructure layer for AI features used inside creative software.

## Recently public / pre-IPO worth watching

- **Canva (private, IPO speculated 2026-2027).** ~$3B ARR, growing 25%+, expanding into enterprise. The structural threat at the bottom of the funnel. When/if it IPOs, will be the largest creative-software listing since Adobe and likely a high-multiple comparator.
- **Runway** (private). Video AI generation; likely IPO candidate medium-term.
- **Midjourney** (private). Image generation; consumer revenue at scale via Discord; enterprise rollout under way.
- **ElevenLabs** (private). Audio/voice generation; enterprise-tilted.

## Notable recent corporate actions

- **Adobe-Figma deal blocked / terminated December 2023.** $1B termination fee paid. Validated regulatory pushback against horizontal consolidation. Figma subsequently IPO'd.
- **ANSYS acquired by Synopsys (2024-2025).** Removed a public CAD/simulation name.
- **Affinity acquired by Canva (2024).** Removed a public-adjacent challenger; gave Canva a pro-tier ladder.
- **Adobe $25B buyback authorization (May 2026).** Largest single buyback authorization in Adobe history; nearly 25% of current market cap.
